Rice Recipe

Description: This is a recipe on how to make tasty rice.

Ingredients

  • Basmati rice
  • Salt
  • Water
  • Garlic
  • Onion
  • Olive oil

Cooking Steps

  1. Cut and dice quarter of an onion.
  2. Dice one clove of garlic.
  3. Add the diced onion and garlic to a saucepan.
  4. Add two tablepoons of olive oil to the saucepan.
  5. Add a pinch of salt.
  6. Turn on the stovetop and set the heat to medium.
  7. Stir everything in the saucepan for about two minutes until the onion and garlic are soft.
  8. Add one cup of basmati rice.
  9. Stir everything in the saucepan for about fourty seconds.
  10. Add boiling water to cover the rice. For every cup of rice add one and a half cups of water.
  11. Leave saucepan on medium heat for ten minutes. Stir gently at five minute mark (optionally add other vegetables such as peas) and then reduce heat to low for the other five minutes.
  12. Rice complete.
